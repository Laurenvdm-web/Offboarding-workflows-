Offboarding Flows

Jetstream Hospitality Solutions

There are two offboarding flows at Jetstream: V1 and V2. The process differs slightly depending on which product the partner is on, but both flows run through the same HubSpot pipeline.

Where to find it

In HubSpot, navigate to the pipeline called Offboarding Pipeline. This is where all offboarding tickets are created and tracked from the initial request through to full completion.

Creating a new ticket — important

When creating a new offboarding ticket, please fill in every field on the ticket form — Property type, Account manager, Property name(s), Property manager, Cirrus URL, and Date of offboarding request. This matters because the form fields drive the entire flow: tasks are automatically assigned to the correct team members and triggered at the right time. If fields are left blank, tasks may not generate correctly or may be assigned to the wrong person.

There are also conditions set both at the form level and within individual stages in HubSpot, so what gets triggered downstream depends directly on what you enter here.

The 4 stages

Stage 1
Offboarding intake. The ticket is created and the intake form is completed. All tasks that can be actioned immediately are assigned automatically at this stage — these are tasks that do not require any guest cancellations or booking handovers to have taken place first. The team can get started on these right away.
Stage 2
Pending cancellations / handovers complete. The ticket should be manually moved to Stage 2 once the last booking has been cancelled or handed over. Tasks are then assigned automatically and will reflect the conditions set in the intake form.
Stage 3
Final cleanup. These are the tasks that can only be actioned right at the end of the offboarding process. They fire automatically when the ticket is moved to this stage. As with Stage 2, some of these tasks are conditional and will only appear based on what was filled in on the intake form.
Stage 4
Offboarding complete. These are the tasks that can only be completed right at the end of the offboarding process. Once all Stage 4 tasks are done, the deal is closed and archived in HubSpot.
